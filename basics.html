Basics

MacroNarr: Main Features

  • Based on previous research: MacroNarr uses constructs established by previous research to identify meaningful units of discourse for analysis. One such example is the Main Concept (MC) analysis by Nicholas and Brookshire (1995).
  • Taking a novel approach: MacroNarr quantifies the frequency of MCs mentioned in succession to determine how typical each chunk of a narrative is.
